how it works

Three steps to protection

No images ever leave your device. We only process a digital fingerprint — a hash — that cannot be reversed into your photo.

1

Select your image

Choose the image you want to protect. It stays on your device — only a fingerprint is ever sent.

2

We generate a hash

Our PDQ algorithm creates a unique fingerprint that matches the image even if rotated, flipped, or re-saved.

3

Platforms are alerted

Partner platforms scan every upload against your hash. Matches are flagged and removed automatically.
